# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 1, 2025**

GQ API ("we", "Company") values your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Types of Information

- **Account info**: Email, username, password hash
- **Usage data**: API call logs, token usage statistics
- **Device info**: IP address, browser type, OS
- **Payment info**: Transaction records (we never store full card numbers)

### 1.2 What We Don't Collect

::: tip Our Commitment
- ❌ We do **NOT store** your prompt (Prompt) content
- ❌ We do **NOT store** AI-generated response content
- ❌ We do **NOT use** your data to train models
- ❌ We do **NOT sell** your personal info to third parties
:::

## 2. How We Use Your Information

Collected data is used solely for:
- Providing, maintaining, and improving our services
- Processing transactions and sending related info
- Monitoring and analyzing usage trends
- Detecting, preventing, and handling fraud
- Complying with legal obligations

## 3. Data Protection

We employ these measures:
- **Transport encryption**: All data transmitted via TLS 1.3
- **Storage encryption**: Sensitive data encrypted with AES-256
- **Access control**: Strict permission management and audit logs
- **Regular audits**: Third-party security audits and penetration testing

## 4. Cookies

Our site may use cookies to:
- Maintain login state
- Analyze site usage
- Provide personalized experience

Manage cookie preferences via browser settings.

## 5. Data Retention

- **Activity logs**: Retained for 90 days
- **Account data**: During account lifetime + 30 days after deletion
- **Financial records**: Per legal requirements

You may request account and data deletion at any time.

## 6. Third-Party Sharing

We don't share your personal info except when:
- We have your explicit consent
- Required by law
- Protecting our/others' rights/safety

All cloud infrastructure providers have signed Data Processing Agreements (DPA).

## 7. International Transfers

Our servers are on secure cloud infrastructure. Cross-border transfers comply with applicable data protection laws.

## 8. Policy Updates

We update this policy periodically. Major changes will be emailed to you.
